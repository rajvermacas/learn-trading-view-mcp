## Summary Rationale

ACUTAAS has a genuinely strong fundamental and technical structure. The weekly and daily trend are firmly bullish — RSI in the 60s across all timeframes, MACD expanding positively, and price trading far above all major EMAs including the 200 EMA (1914). The stock is up approximately 58% from the January low of 1570 and is now testing its all-time high zone of 2634.

**The core challenge for the 3% stop screen is the ATR problem.** With daily ATR at 117 (4.5% of CMP), the entire 2–4% practical stop zone (2496–2548) represents less than one day's normal range. The stock can and does move 100–150 points in a single session regularly (see Apr 1: high 2635, low 2233 — a 402-point range; Apr 28: 80-point range intraday). This makes mechanical 3% stops highly susceptible to intraday noise without any structural break.

**The 3% floor at 2522 specifically sits in a zone with no clean anchoring support:** it is above the Apr 27–29 daily lows (2460–2462) but below the recent swing highs (2539). There is no significant pivot, EMA, or volume node precisely at 2522. The **4% floor at 2496 is better anchored** — it aligns with the Apr 28–29 daily congestion base, the 30m 50 EMA (2506), and represents the floor of the recent multi-day basing zone.

**Verdict is Near-Valid**: the structural trend is unequivocally bullish and the fundamental sponsorship is strong (#9 ranked, CDMO moat). However, the stop zone defense depends entirely on using the wider end (4% / 2496) rather than the 3% reference. A tight 3% stop at 2522 will likely be triggered by normal daily volatility before any actual structural breakdown. Traders should either accept the 4% stop (~2496) or wait for a lower-risk entry on a pullback to the S3–S4 zone (2460–2500) before initiating.
