## Notes on Ranking Discipline

1. **Fundamental rank came first** — all 25 stocks were fundamentally analyzed before any technical work began.
2. **Technical rank only resolves ties and confirms/denies the trade** — a weaker fundamental stock cannot jump above a stronger one due to technical setup alone.
3. **LLOYDSME ranks #2 combined** despite being #3 fundamentally because its technical label (Best Aligned) confirms the setup with higher conviction than PREMIERENE's Near-Valid (which requires a retracement entry to validate).
4. **Technical review not run** does not mean rejection — these stocks may be valid setups. It means the user-specified coverage limit (top 5 technical) was applied.
5. **All Near-Valid names require pullback entries** — entry at CMP for PREMIERENE, GVT&D, or ATLANTAELE today carries direct stop-band exposure with no structural cushion.
