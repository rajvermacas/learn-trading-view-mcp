- **Primary failing dimension**: Most recent quarter is trough-level earnings; rally is forward-looking; catalyst timeline mismatches with 1–8 week swing window.

### KRISHANA — Krishana Phoschem Ltd
- **Verdict**: Mixed
- **Reason**: 50% DAP/NPK capacity expansion commissioned March 2026 is real and datable. Q4 FY26 PAT ₹83 Cr (+153% YoY) headline is striking. However, FY26 operating cash flow was **negative ₹191 Cr** despite ₹180 Cr reported PAT — a first-order earnings quality signal. Q4 PAT was inflated by a negative effective tax rate (-14.64%), adding ~₹11 Cr that will not recur. Trade receivables doubled to ₹715 Cr. Management explicitly stated "FY26 margin levels may not be fully sustainable in FY27." Short-term borrowings nearly tripled. At an all-time high with these structural vulnerabilities, the risk-reward is unfavorable for a new swing entry.
- **Primary failing dimension**: PAT–CFO divergence is severe (₹180 Cr PAT vs -₹191 Cr CFO). Earnings quality does not support the headline growth rate.
- **Best support near stop zone**: Not assessed — technical review not run.
- **Re-check candidate**: Conditional. Recheck if Q1 FY27 shows (a) positive CFO, (b) OPM stabilization at 12%+, and (c) receivables normalizing below 80 days. Without those, the quality concern persists at any price.
- **Technical review status**: Technical review not run in this execution.
