- **Recent Trigger And What Likely Drove The Move**: The 3-month rally of 39.27% from approximately Jan–Apr 2026 was driven by four identifiable events in sequence: (1) Q3 FY26 results and maiden earnings call (Feb 2026) — first post-IPO earnings call, 17.76% revenue growth confirmed, management provided EBITDA guidance of 26-27% and bed expansion roadmap to 5,260 by FY28; (2) KPIMS Agra acquisition (360-bed, ₹245 Cr) and associated SVPD/KPS Wellness closures in Jan-Mar 2026 confirmed IPO use-of-proceeds execution; (3) Nuvama coverage initiation at ₹280 Buy in March 2026 catalysed institutional interest from a ₹202 base; (4) Panchkula hospital launch (Apr 10, 2026) and Tricity expansion narrative.   7. Revenue CAGR of 9% (3-year) and profit CAGR of 5% (3-year) are modest — the 44x P/E is justified only if the FY26-FY28 acceleration (Nuvama's 24% revenue CAGR, 28% PAT CAGR) actually materialises; there is no historical proof of this acceleration rate.
  8. IPO lock-in: Promoters at 82.89% — any indication of secondary sale post lock-in expiry creates overhang.

Among the broader 3-month screen universe, PARKHOSPS ranks well on catalyst density and confirmed IPO deployment, but should rank below peers with confirmed earnings acceleration (not guided) and below those with more balanced payor mixes. It should rank above pure momentum names with no fundamental anchor.
