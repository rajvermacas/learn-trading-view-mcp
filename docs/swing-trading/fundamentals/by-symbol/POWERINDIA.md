### Sponsorship Reasoning

- **Why Sponsorship Is Present Or Not**: Sponsorship is present but has shifted from "Strongly Sponsored" (prior dossier) to "Moderately Sponsored" for the 1–8 week swing window. The fundamental sponsorship is unchanged; the evidence-to-price alignment has deteriorated. The swing entry case is weaker at Rs 33,730 than it was at Rs 28,925.

No new large order announcement has been disclosed.

The long-term bull case is intact; the near-term entry is more stretched.

- **Contradictions, Weaknesses, And Open Risks**:
  1. **Valuation further stretched**: P/E 170x (Screener) to ~210–220x (trailing if Q4 PAT is Rs 65–80 Cr). The gap vs peers has widened since Apr 19.
  2. **Stock +16.6% since Apr 19 on no new fundamental data**: The incremental move is momentum-driven. Pure momentum extension at this valuation is a risk factor, not a supporting argument.
  3. **Q4 FY26 results imminent (mid-May)**: The 70–75% sequential PAT decline expected by consensus will arrive within the next 2 weeks. At Rs 33,730 CMP (vs Rs 28,925 when this risk was last assessed), the downside exposure on a weak Q4 is higher.
