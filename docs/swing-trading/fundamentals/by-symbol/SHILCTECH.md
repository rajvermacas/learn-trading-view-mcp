The 3-month price move was driven by a confluence: India's renewable energy capacity addition in 9MFY26 (34.7 GW) surpassed the entire FY25 addition (28.7 GW), creating an immediate surge in transformer demand. SHILCTECH as a leading distribution and power transformer manufacturer is a direct beneficiary. Layered on top: Q3 FY26 earnings beat (PAT +22% YoY to ₹42.34 Cr, revenue +11% YoY to ₹170.26 Cr) and the 1:1 bonus share announcement. Order pipeline of ₹750–800 Cr for FY26 provides near-term revenue visibility.
