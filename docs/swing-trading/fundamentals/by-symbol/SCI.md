## Sponsorship Reasoning

**Why Sponsorship Is Present Or Not:** Present but qualified. 3M momentum not a mystery — clear, multi-layered business foundation: tanker freight rate recovery, VLGC fleet additions, Vision 2047 strategic repositioning, strong Q3 FY26 result that genuinely beat prior-year levels by operating margin expansion, not accounting tricks. "Moderately" rather than "Strongly" because core profit driver (tanker cycle) inherently mean-reverting; 436% YoY partly low-base artifact; ROCE 9.81% reflects capital-intensive business earning below cost of capital in normalized cycle conditions.

**Recent Trigger And What Likely Drove The Move:** Primary trigger Q3 FY26 results released February 2026, showing ₹405 Cr net profit (+436% YoY) and ₹1,612 Cr revenue (+22.5% YoY). Market re-rated with reports of 14% single-day surge on result day. Secondary triggers sustaining momentum: (a) SCI inducted VLGC Sahyadri and VLGC Shivalik (Hyundai-built, 82,000 cbm each) Q3/Q4 FY26 on Persian Gulf-India route; (b) MoA for additional second-hand VLGC acquisitions FY26; (c) March 2026 shipbuilding pact with Mazagon Dock for methanol dual-fuel PSV — tangible green shipping progress; (d) Government formally repositioned SCI as strategic national carrier post-privatisation abandonment May 2025, opening JV formation with IOC/BPCL/HPCL/ONGC. Macro backdrop — India's crude oil import volumes, energy security priorities — provides persistent demand for tanker services.

**Business And Financial Evidence:** Quarterly progression (consolidated): Dec 2025 (Q3 FY26) revenue ₹1,612 Cr, PAT ₹405 Cr, OPM 42%; Sep 2025 (Q2 FY26) ₹1,339 Cr, ₹189 Cr, 30%; Jun 2025 (Q1 FY26) ₹1,316 Cr, ₹354 Cr, 37%; Mar 2025 (Q4 FY25) ₹1,325 Cr, ₹185 Cr, 28%; Dec 2024 (Q3 FY25) ₹1,316 Cr, ₹76 Cr, 27% ← low base. Q3 FY25 profit ₹76 Cr itself anomaly — 44% below Q3 FY24's ₹134 Cr — driven by tanker rate weakness and bulk carrier softness. Q3 FY26 recovery to ₹405 Cr genuine recovery but against artificially depressed base.
