## Sponsorship Reasoning

**Why Sponsorship Is Present Or Not**: Sponsorship is present but not clean. The Q3 FY26 consolidated earnings release in early February 2026 triggered a 20% upper circuit, the first time in eight quarters OPM exceeded 10%. The order book at ₹8,709 Cr with 18.7% YoY growth and 85% private-sector client mix signals genuine demand visibility. However, earnings quality is poor: OCF collapsed 84% YoY in FY25, FCF went negative, and working capital swallowed ₹375 Cr as reported profits grew. Market is pricing structural inflection; 9M FY26 evidence suggests single-quarter beat, not durable turn. Mixed reflects gap between what is proven (one strong quarter, intact order book) and what is assumed (sustained OPM improvement, FCF recovery).

**Recent Trigger And What Likely Drove The Move**: Stock traded near ₹769 before Q3 FY26 result. Stock locked 20% upper circuit at ₹946, then rose to ₹1,042 by April 2026, a ~35% post-trigger move. Weak Q3 FY25 base amplified percentage surge optics. Sector re-rating of infrastructure/capital goods provided amplifying backdrop.
